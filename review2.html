Movie Info

Brilliant parapsychologist Elise Rainier receives a disturbing phone call
from a man who claims that his house is haunted. Even more disturbing is
the address -- 413 Apple Tree Lane in Five Keys, N.M. -- the home where
Elise grew up as a child. Accompanied by her two investigative partners,
Rainier travels to Five Keys to confront and destroy her greatest fear --
the demon that she accidentally set free years earlier.

Rating : PG-13 (for disturbing thematic content, violence and terror, and brief strong language)
