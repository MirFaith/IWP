Movie Info

Inspired by the imagination of P.T. Barnum, The Greatest Showman is an original musical
that celebrates the birth of show business & tells of a visionary who rose
from nothing to create a spectacle that became a worldwide sensation.

Rating : PG (for thematic elements including a brawl)
Genre : Drama, Musical & Performing Arts
Directed By : Michael Gracey
Written By : Jenny Bicks, Bill Condon
In Theaters : Dec 20, 2017 Wide
Runtime : 105 minutes
Studio : 20th Century Fox

Cast

Zac Efron as Phillip Carlyle

Zendaya as Annie

Michelle Williams as Charity Barnum

Rebecca Ferguson as Jenny Lind
