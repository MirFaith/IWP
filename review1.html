Movie Info

From the Marvel Cinematic Universe comes a new chapter featuring
heroes with the astonishing ability to shrink: "Ant-Man and The Wasp."
In the aftermath of "Captain America: Civil War," Scott Lang (Rudd)
grapples with the consequences of his choices as both a Super Hero and a father.
As he struggles to rebalance his home life with his responsibilities as Ant-Man,
he's confronted by Hope van Dyne (Lilly) and Dr. Hank Pym (Douglas) with an urgent
new mission. Scott must once again put on the suit and learn to fight alongside
The Wasp as the team works together to uncover secrets from their past.
